# 🧠 Product Strategy

## 🎯 Vision

Help people **control sugar intake daily** through real-time, intelligent guidance.

---

## 🧭 Positioning

NOT:

* Generic calorie tracker
* Basic food scanner

BUT:

* Sugar-focused decision assistant

---

## 👥 Target Users

* Health-conscious individuals
* Parents managing children’s diet
* People with diabetes or weight goals

---

## 🏆 Competitive Advantage

1. Deep sugar intelligence
2. Family-based tracking
3. Camera-first UX
4. Real-time decision support

---

## 🔥 Core Value Proposition

"Know before you consume."

---

## 📊 Success Metrics

* Daily active users (DAU)
* Scan-to-decision rate
* User retention (7-day, 30-day)
* Avg sugar tracked per user

---

## 💰 Monetization (Future)

* Premium insights
* Family plans
* Health integrations

---

## ⚠️ Risks

* Data accuracy
* Behavior change difficulty
* API dependency

---

## 🧠 Strategy Principle

Focus on:
👉 Decision-making, not data display
